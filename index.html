How it works?

This dashboard provides a comprehensive and easy-to-understand overview of your household energy usage, allowing you to track consumption in real time and over longer periods. It presents key information through simple visual elements such as charts, progress indicators, and summaries, helping you quickly identify patterns and trends in your energy behaviour.
The dashboard highlights important details such as expected costs and remaining energy, supporting more informed decision-making.
Overall, the platform is designed to simplify complex energy data into clear, actionable insights, empowering users to reduce unnecessary consumption, manage expenses more effectively, and build more sustainable energy habits without feeling overwhelmed.
